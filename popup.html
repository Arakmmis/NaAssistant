Welcome to the assistant. Press the Refresh! button to start! There are 2 tabs, Characters and Mission tabs. You can refresh each tab by pressing their refresh button. If you encounter a bug please message me, if the extension freezes then reinstalling does the trick. If a new character is released or a balance update has happened then press refresh to repopulate the list. If you have completed a new mission, press Get Missions button to refresh. Refreshing may take a while!
During battle you can select a character from this list to check out their abilties/chakra/cooldowns.
